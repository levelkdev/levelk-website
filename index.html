Decentralize
Using blockchain technology, Level K is changing the way people organize.

Our team is passionately working towards building token based economies, promoting decentralization in our community, and advancing blockchain technology.

  • Community

    We started Boston Ethereum Developers for anyone interested in building Dapps on Ethereum. Members meet monthly to share projects and discuss decentralization.

  • Projects

    We are creating a decentralized platform for open source bounty programs. You can find our open source projects on github.com/levelkdev.

  • Slack

    Joining our community Slack is the best way to get in touch with us. We're there to chat with all who are interested.

  • Conferences

    We'll be at M-0 in Zug, Switzerland and at Devcon3 in Cancun, Mexico. Drop us a line if you would like to meet us there.
